Banana Muffins

There delicious banana muffins are easy for kids to make

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 25 mins
  • Total: 35 mins
  • Servings: 12
  • Yield: 12 Muffins, or 48 mini muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large bananas. mashed
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup butter. melted
